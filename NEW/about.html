Our Story

The Pinson Family &
Whiskey Creek Beach NW

The Whiskey Creek Beach NW property was purchased in 2017 by the Pinson family, and has since become a spectacular destination for peaceful getaways, family reunions, and even a few weddings.

Located just 25 minutes outside Port Angeles, guests can enjoy the serenity and beauty of the Olympic Peninsula with easy access to town and some of the best hiking, hunting, fishing, and exploring you will ever find.

There is so much to do in and around the Olympic National Forest — so take your time, breathe the salt air, and enjoy your stay.

The Property

One Stunning
Oceanfront Property

Whiskey Creek Beach NW sits right on the Strait of Juan de Fuca, offering direct beach access from every accommodation. The property spans from the forested hillside down to the rocky shoreline.

Walk the private beach, use the boat ramp for kayaking or fishing on the Strait, gather around a beachfront fire pit in the evening, and wake up to bald eagles soaring overhead.

The Setting

A Private Beach
All Your Own

Few places offer this level of shoreline access. At Whiskey Creek, the beach is part of your stay — not a destination you drive to. Explore the tide flats, spot seals and otters, and watch for migrating whales along the Strait.

Things To Do

What Awaits You Here

Beachfront Bonfires

Gather around a fire with family and friends as the tide comes in. Bonfire evenings on the Strait are something you'll remember for years.

Explore & Hike

From ancient rainforests to alpine ridgelines, Olympic National Park offers world-class trails just minutes from the property. Day trips for every ability level.

Wildlife & Water

Launch a kayak from the private boat ramp, fish the Strait, or simply watch bald eagles, seals, and the occasional whale pass by from your own stretch of beach.
