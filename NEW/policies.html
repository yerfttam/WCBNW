Our Policies

Booking, Cancellation & Pet Policies

Good to Know

Booking Policies

Please review our policies before booking. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to contact us directly at (844) 769-2322 or whiskeycreekbeachnw@gmail.com.

Important: When booking, be sure your email address is correct as you will receive your booking confirmation and detailed self check-in instructions. Please check your spam or junk mail folder if you don't see it.

Damage Deposit

A $150 damage deposit is charged to your credit card 24 hours prior to your booking for all cabin and RV rentals.

The deposit is automatically refunded after your visit if no damage is reported. If damage occurs, you will be contacted directly.

Cancellation Policies

Cabins & RV Rentals

  • Full refund for cancellations made 60 or more days prior to arrival.
  • 50% refund for cancellations made up to 30 days prior to arrival (service fees excluded).
  • Same policy applies to re-scheduling requests.

Tent Sites & RV Sites

  • Full refund if you cancel within 48 hours of booking, provided check-in is at least 14 days away.
  • 50% refund for cancellations made at least 7 days before check-in.
  • No refunds for cancellations or re-schedules within 7 days of check-in.

Pet Policy

We love pets! Five pet-friendly cabins and one pet-friendly RV rental are available.

Larger Pets Welcome (max 2)

  • Halibut Hole and Captains Quarters accept larger pets, maximum 2.
  • Whiskey Creek Beach RV and Eagles Nest RV also accept larger pets.

Small Pets (under 25 lbs, max 1)

  • Jasper Inn, Barnacle Hill, and Codfish Cottage accept 1 pet under 25 lbs.
  • Exceptions may be made for guests not using couch beds for sleeping.

Bedding & Pet Fees

  • $25 flat fee when bringing your own bedding (only sheets are provided).
  • Additional charges may apply for larger, heavy-shedding animals.

Properties That Do Not Allow Pets

  • Beachcomber Cottage
  • Prevost Luxury Coach
  • Hillcrest Cabin
  • Cedar Heights

Check-In & Check-Out

  • Cabin & Cottage check-in: 4:00 PM. Check-out: 11:00 AM.
  • Tent & RV site check-in: 2:00 PM. Check-out: 11:00 AM.
  • Self check-in instructions are emailed after booking — verify your email address is correct.
  • Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.
