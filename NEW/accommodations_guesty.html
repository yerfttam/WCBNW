Waterfront Accommodations

Beach Front Cabins

Five individual cabins set directly on the shoreline of the Strait of Juan de Fuca. Each cabin offers a private beachfront experience with stunning ocean views and the sound of waves steps from your door.

Private Beach,
Unobstructed Views

Wake up to the sound of waves on the Strait of Juan de Fuca. Each beachfront cabin sits directly on the shoreline of one of the most scenic stretches of the Olympic Peninsula.

Cozy & Unique Stays

Cottages

Six distinct cottages ranging from ocean-view hideaways to charming tiny homes and a luxury converted tour bus. Every cottage offers unique character and the same breathtaking coastal setting.

Open Air Shelters

A-Frame Shelters

Eight A-frame shelters positioned along the oceanfront with incredible views of the Strait. A step above tent camping — bring your sleeping gear and wake up to the sound of the Pacific.

Camp Under
the Open Sky

Tent sites and A-frame shelters offer the most immersive coastal camping experience on the Olympic Peninsula — with direct beach access and unobstructed views of the Strait.

Beachfront Camping

Tent Sites

Six ocean-view tent sites set along the beach. Bring your own tent and gear and fall asleep to the sound of waves. Sites T1–T4 and T12 offer open views; T14 is a premier beachfront campsite.

Full Hookup RV Camping

RV Sites

Three ocean-view RV sites with full hookups. Bring your rig and enjoy the same stunning coastal setting as the cabins — with the convenience of your home on wheels. Sites B1, B2, and C1 available.
